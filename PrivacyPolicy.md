# Privacy Policy for BetterSplit

_Last updated: September 28, 2025_

BetterSplit ("we", "our", or "us") is a bill splitting app designed to help friends easily track and settle shared expenses. We value your privacy and want you to understand how we handle your information.  

---

## 1. Offline-Only Design
BetterSplit is a **completely offline app**.  
- We do not require an internet connection for core features.  
- All calculations and data processing happen **directly on your device**.  
- We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

---

## 2. Data We Handle
BetterSplit may temporarily handle the following information locally on your device:  
- **Trip details**: expense amounts, participant names, and notes you enter.  
- **QR codes**: generated to share trip and expense details with your friends.  
- **Final QR code**: created by merging shared trip data for the final split.

This data remains under your control and is not shared with us or any third parties.

---

## 3. Data Sharing
- When you share trip details, they are transferred **only via QR codes** you generate and scan.  
- No information is sent over the internet.  
- You choose what to share and with whom.  

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
BetterSplit does **not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services**.  
There are no hidden connections to external servers.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
- All data stays **on your device** until you delete it.  
- Uninstalling the app will remove all stored information.  
- You have full control over what is stored and for how long.

---

## 6. Security
Because all data is stored locally and not uploaded anywhere, your privacy risk is minimized.  
We recommend keeping your device secure (e.g., using a passcode or biometric lock) to protect your personal information.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy
BetterSplit does not knowingly collect or process any personal information from children. The app is designed for general use and requires no personal accounts.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or via an updated version of this document.
